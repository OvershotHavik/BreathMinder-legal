Privacy Policy for BreathMinder

Effective date: April 9, 2026

BreathMinder (“BreathMinder,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is operated by Steven Plavetzky Jr. This Privacy Policy explains how BreathMinder handles information when you use the BreathMinder mobile application (the “App”).

1. Overview

BreathMinder is a wellness app designed to help users with guided breathing and related wellness features. BreathMinder is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and is intended for general wellness and informational purposes only.

2. Information We Collect

At this time, BreathMinder is designed so that user data is primarily stored locally on the user’s device.

Depending on the features you use, the App may access or store the following categories of information:

  • breathing session settings and usage history
  • app preferences and notification preferences
  • custom affirmations, custom styles, and related user-created content
  • photos or images you choose to import into the App
  • journal entries and other wellness notes you choose to create in the App
  • HealthKit data that you explicitly authorize the App to read or write

3. Information We Do Not Collect for Our Own Servers

At this time:

  • we do not require account creation
  • we do not collect your email address through in-app sign-up
  • we do not sell your personal information
  • we do not serve third-party ads
  • we do not transmit your app data to our own servers for storage

BreathMinder does not currently operate its own backend for storing your personal wellness data.

4. HealthKit Information

If you choose to connect Apple Health / HealthKit, BreathMinder may read and/or write Health data only for the specific data types you authorize.

HealthKit data is used solely to support app features you request, such as viewing or logging wellness-related information. We do not use HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or unrelated profiling.

You can manage HealthKit permissions at any time in the Apple Health app or your device settings.

5. How Your Information Is Used

BreathMinder uses information stored in or made available to the App to:

  • provide and operate app features
  • save your breathing preferences and customizations
  • display your session history and progress
  • support optional journaling and personalization features
  • support optional HealthKit-related features you authorize
  • process subscription access through Apple’s in-app purchase system
  • respond if you choose to contact us for support or feedback

6. Payments and Subscriptions

All purchases and subscription transactions in BreathMinder are handled by Apple through Apple’s in-app purchase system. We do not receive or store your full payment card information.

Your purchases are subject to Apple’s terms, billing practices, and privacy practices.

7. User Content

You may choose to create or import content within the App, including custom affirmations, custom styles, journal content, and photos.

You are responsible for the content you create, upload, import, or store in the App, and for ensuring you have the necessary rights to use that content.

8. Data Storage

At this time, BreathMinder stores data locally on your device. Some app data may also be included in device-level backups or Apple-managed services depending on your device settings and Apple platform behavior.

BreathMinder may add optional iCloud sync or similar Apple-managed synchronization features in the future. If that occurs, this Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect those changes.

9. Diagnostics and Analytics

BreathMinder does not currently use third-party analytics or crash-reporting SDKs. In the future, we may use Apple-provided or other privacy-conscious diagnostic tools to improve the App. If we do, we may update this Privacy Policy accordingly.

If you voluntarily contact us for support or feedback, you may choose to include diagnostic details, screenshots, or other information in your message.

10. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your app data with third parties for their independent advertising purposes.

We may disclose information only:

  • if required by law
  • to protect rights, safety, or the integrity of the App
  • in connection with a business transfer, merger, or reorganization, if one ever occurs

11. Children’s Privacy

BreathMinder is not directed specifically to children. Because the App does not currently require account creation or collect personal information to our own servers in the ordinary course of use, our direct collection of personal data is limited.

Parents or guardians who have questions may contact us using the email below.

12. International Users

BreathMinder may be made available to users in multiple countries. By using the App, you understand that your use of Apple platform services may involve processing under Apple’s systems and policies applicable to your region.

13. Your Choices

You can generally control your information by:

  • editing or deleting content you store in the App
  • deleting the App from your device
  • managing notification permissions in device settings
  • managing HealthKit permissions in Apple Health and device settings
  • managing subscription status through your Apple account settings

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Effective date” above. Continued use of the App after changes become effective means you accept the revised Privacy Policy.
